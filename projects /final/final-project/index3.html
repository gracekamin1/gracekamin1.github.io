Statistics


According to a study done by PRS global open, 84% of participants that previously underwent a cosmetic procedure said they were influenced by social media, and 69% of participants that were interested in a cosmetic procedure were influenced by social media. The study results also showed that 51% of participants followed social media accounts of plastic surgeons.
